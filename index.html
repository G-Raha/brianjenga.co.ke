Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about my work, timelines, collaboration, and outcomes.

(1. What problems do you typically solve?
I help teams increase qualified organic traffic, clarify positioning, and improve conversion. That ranges from SEO copywriting and content strategy to website SEO audits and GA4/Looker reporting. See the full list on the Services page.
(2. Who do you work best with?
B2B SaaS, healthcare, sustainability, and professional services. I also support founders and solo teams that need a senior partner to set direction and ship reliably.
(3. How do we get started?
Start with a short discovery call and a light audit of your goals and current content. You’ll receive a scoped proposal with timelines, milestones, and pricing. Reach out here.
(4. What are your typical timelines?
Small deliverables (e.g., a landing page or mini audit) take 5–10 business days. Strategy programs and content calendars run 4–8 weeks. Large site refreshes depend on scope and team availability.
(5. How do you measure success?
We align on KPIs up front and track them in GA4 and Looker Studio: qualified sessions, engaged sessions, key events (CTA clicks, downloads), assisted conversions, and revenue proxies. We annotate changes so cause and effect are visible.
(6. What does collaboration look like week to week?
Expect an agreed cadence (weekly or bi-weekly), shared docs, and transparent task boards. I work async across time zones from Mombasa, Kenya, and keep feedback cycles tight.
(7. Do you handle implementation or just make recommendations?
Both. I can deliver strategy and content and implement on-page fixes: metadata, headings, internal links, content refreshes, and liaison with devs for technical items.
(8. What’s your revision policy?
Proposals include a defined number of revision rounds focused on the agreed brief and KPIs. Additional changes are scoped fairly to protect timelines and quality.
