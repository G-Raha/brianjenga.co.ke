Omnichannel Starter Stack for Small Brands

A pocket guide for small brands to move from scattered channels to a connected customer journey—without the overwhelm.

TL;DR
  • What it is: A lean starter stack to unify email, store, CRM, and ads so small brands can run a connected journey without bloat.
  • What’s inside: Must-have tools by category, simple automation/workflows, budget-based platform pairings, and a 3-month rollout plan.
  • Roadmap: Month 1 (core setup) → Month 2 (connect & automate) → Month 3 (personalize & expand with referrals/community).
  • Results: Better retention, cleaner data, and consistent experiences across touchpoints—focus on the channels that matter.
  • Stack needed: Email/CRM + automation + storefront + lightweight analytics; start small, upgrade in tiers as you grow.

What’s inside

  1. Must-Have Tools for Integration: Email + CRM (Klaviyo, MailerLite, HubSpot Free), automation (Zapier, Make.com), storefronts (Shopify, WooCommerce), support tools (Gorgias, ManyChat, Tawk.to), and analytics (GA4, Hotjar, Triple Whale).
  2. Simple Workflows: Cart abandonment loops, ad retargeting paths, smart segmentation, and customer journey recaps.
  3. Platform Pairings by Budget: Bootstrap (MailerLite + Etsy/Gumroad), Growth (Klaviyo + Shopify + Hotjar), Scale-Up (HubSpot + Shopify apps + paid social + Triple Whale).

3-Month Roadmap:

  • Month 1: Audit & align journey, choose 3 core platforms, sync branding.
  • Month 2: Connect email + store, launch retargeting, automate cart recovery.
  • Month 3: Add personalization, survey customers, expand into referral/community.

Final Tip: The best brands aren’t everywhere—they’re consistent in the channels that matter most.

FAQs: Omnichannel Starter Stack

What is the Omnichannel Starter Stack?

A curated toolkit that helps small brands unify their marketing channels with affordable tools, simple workflows, and a 3-month rollout plan.

Who should use this toolkit?

Solopreneurs, small e-commerce shops, and growth-stage teams that want a lean, connected customer journey without bloated tech spend.

Do I need expensive software to get started?

No. The toolkit offers budget tiers (Bootstrap, Growth, Scale-Up) with affordable entry points like MailerLite, Gumroad, or Shopify plugins.

How long does it take to set up?

The roadmap is designed for 3 months—Month 1: core setup, Month 2: connect and automate, Month 3: personalize and expand.

Can I adapt this if I’m B2B instead of e-commerce?

Yes. The workflows (cart recovery, segmentation, referrals) translate to lead nurturing, CRM integration, and service-based journeys.
