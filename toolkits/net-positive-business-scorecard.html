Net Positive Business Scorecard

Move beyond Net Zero. Use this scorecard to benchmark regenerative impact, equity, and well-being—and chart a path to Net Positive leadership.

TL;DR
  • What it is: A Net Positive scorecard to measure five dimensions—Regenerative Impact, Community Well-being, Equitable Governance, Knowledge & Capacity-Building, and Multi-Metric Success.
  • How to score: Use a simple 0–3 rubric—from “not addressed” to “fully embedded”—then total out of 15.
  • Interpretation: 0–5 Foundations Needed • 6–10 Growing Impact • 11–15 Fully Net Positive.
  • Next steps: Target the lowest dimension, co-design actions with affected communities, and revisit annually to track progress.
  • Use cases: ESG reviews, stakeholder workshops, and reporting—can be a fillable PDF or 1-page leadership grid.

What’s inside

Five Core Dimensions:

  1. Regenerative Impact — restore ecosystems, biodiversity, and resources.
  2. Community Well-being— improve livelihoods, dignity, and resilience.
  3. Equitable Governance — share decision-making power, ensure inclusion, and practice transparency.
  4. Knowledge & Capacity-Building— leave stakeholders more capable and empowered.
  5. Multi-Metric Success— measure outcomes beyond profit or emissions, from mental health to cultural preservation.

Scoring Guide: Rate each dimension from 0 (not addressed) to 3 (fully embedded).

Interpretation:

  • 0–5: Foundations Needed.
  • 6–10: Growing Impact.
  • 11–15: Fully Net Positive.

Next Steps: Identify gaps, co-design with communities, and revisit annually to track progress.

FAQs: Net Positive Business Scorecard

What is a Net Positive Business Scorecard?

It’s a framework that helps businesses measure regenerative impact, community well-being, equitable governance, knowledge-building, and multi-metric success.

How is this different from Net Zero?

Net Zero focuses on reducing harm (mainly emissions). Net Positive goes further—creating environmental, social, and cultural benefits that outweigh business footprints.

Who should use this scorecard?

Sustainability officers, CSR professionals, ESG strategists, founders, and any business aiming to move beyond compliance into regenerative leadership.

How do I score my company?

Rate each of the five dimensions from 0 (not addressed) to 3 (fully embedded). Use the total score to benchmark progress and identify improvement areas.

Does this toolkit support reporting?

Yes. The scorecard can be used in sustainability reports, board reviews, and stakeholder workshops to communicate impact beyond financials.
